Academic profile

My name is Lucy Visser and I am a student in the Research Master Ancient, Medieval and Renaissance Studies at Utrecht University, where I previously completed a BA in History. I specializes in Ancient Studies with a focus on the Etruscan civilization, notably the intersections of religion and gender.
This has culminated in my BA Thesis on social difference in Etruscan funerary culture. My interest in religion and gender also extends to Roman and Greek history. In future research I intend to contribute to re-evaluating the Etruscan civilization, in order better understand its role within the Mediterranean context.
